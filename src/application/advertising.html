Maintain strong brand presence 2. Create memorable, impactful designs 3. Ensure clear messaging 4. Include strong calls-to-action 5. Test effectiveness ## Digital Ads Guidelines for creating digital advertisements that effectively communicate our brand. ### Design Principles - Clear, compelling visuals - Strong brand presence - Readable typography - Effective use of color - Clear call-to-action ### Best Practices 1. Keep designs simple and focused 2. Use high-quality imagery 3. Ensure brand consistency 4. Test across different platforms 5. Optimize for various ad sizes ## Print Ads Guidelines for creating print advertisements that represent our brand effectively. ### Design Principles - Strong visual hierarchy - Clear messaging - Brand-aligned imagery - Appropriate use of white space - Compelling call-to-action ### Best Practices 1. Use high-resolution images (300 DPI) 2. Maintain brand consistency 3. Ensure readability 4. Test print quality 5. Consider publication context ## Billboards Guidelines for creating billboard advertisements that make a strong brand impact. ### Design Principles - Large, readable text - Simple, clear messaging - Strong visual impact - High contrast for visibility - Minimal text for quick reading ### Best Practices 1. Keep messaging extremely simple 2. Use large, bold typography 3. Ensure visibility from distance 4. Use high contrast colors 5. Test readability at various distances
