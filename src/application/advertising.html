Our advertising should reinforce our reputation as a reliable, professional service provider. ## Advertising Principles 1. Maintain strong brand presence emphasizing our tagline and key messages 2. Create memorable, impactful designs that convey professionalism and reliability 3. Ensure clear messaging about our services and 24/7 availability 4. Include strong calls-to-action: "Online Inquiry / Product Order" or "Get in Touch" 5. Test effectiveness across different platforms and audiences 6. Emphasize our service excellence and reliability in all advertising ## Digital Ads Guidelines for creating digital advertisements that effectively communicate Moffitt Services brand and drive inquiries for fuel services, lubricants, disaster response, and other professional services. ### Design Principles - Clear, compelling visuals showcasing our services (fuel trucks, service vehicles, disaster response) - Strong brand presence with logo and tagline "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™" - Readable typography emphasizing key messages: 24/7 availability, reliability, service excellence - Effective use of brand colors maintaining professional appearance - Clear call-to-action: "Online Inquiry / Product Order" or "Get in Touch Now" ### Best Practices 1. Keep designs simple and focused on service benefits 2. Use high-quality imagery of our fleet and services 3. Ensure brand consistency across all digital platforms 4. Test across different platforms (Google Ads, social media, industry websites) 5. Optimize for various ad sizes while maintaining brand visibility 6. Emphasize our 24/7 availability and national reach 7. Target industry-specific audiences (Agriculture, Construction, Pipeline & Oil, Emergency, Manufacturing, Railroad) ## Print Ads Guidelines for creating print advertisements that represent Moffitt Services effectively in trade publications, industry magazines, and local publications. ### Design Principles - Strong visual hierarchy emphasizing our services and tagline - Clear messaging about 24/7 availability and service excellence - Brand-aligned imagery showcasing professional service delivery - Appropriate use of white space for professional, clean appearance - Compelling call-to-action: "Online Inquiry / Product Order" or contact information ### Best Practices 1. Use high-resolution images (300 DPI minimum) of our services and fleet 2. Maintain brand consistency with logo, colors, and typography 3. Ensure readability, especially for contact information 4. Test print quality before final production 5. Consider publication context - target industry-specific publications 6. Include key messages: "24/7, 365 days a year" and "Ensuring maximum performance with no downtime" 7. Feature location-specific information when advertising in regional publications ## Billboards Guidelines for creating billboard advertisements that make a strong Moffitt Services brand impact, especially effective near major highways, industrial areas, and locations where our target industries operate. ### Design Principles - Large, readable text featuring our tagline or key message - Simple, clear messaging: "24/7 Fuel Delivery" or "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™" - Strong visual impact with logo and service imagery - High contrast for visibility day and night - Minimal text for quick reading at highway speeds ### Best Practices 1. Keep messaging extremely simple - focus on one key message 2. Use large, bold typography that's readable from 500+ feet 3. Ensure visibility from distance - test at various viewing angles 4. Use high contrast colors from our brand palette 5. Test readability at various distances before final production 6. Include phone number (+1 (281) 205-7577) in large, clear font 7. Consider location-specific messaging (near industrial areas, highways, etc.) 8. Use reflective or illuminated materials for 24/7 visibility
