--- layout: layouts/base.njk title: Writing Style Guide description: Guidelines for written communication breadcrumbs: - label: Home url: / - label: Voice url: /voice - label: Writing Style Guide url: /voice/writing-style-guide category: Voice --- ## Writing Style Guide This guide provides specific rules and recommendations for written communications. ## Grammar and Style ### Active Voice Prefer active voice over passive voice for clarity and impact. **Good**: "We deliver exceptional service." **Avoid**: "Exceptional service is delivered by us." ### Sentence Length Keep sentences concise and clear. Aim for 15-20 words on average. ### Paragraph Length Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences) for better readability, especially online. ## Word Choice ### Preferred Terms - **Service-focused language**: "service," "reliability," "professional," "exceptional" - **Action-oriented words**: "deliver," "ensure," "provide," "guarantee" - **Clear, simple language**: Avoid unnecessary complexity - **Industry-appropriate terms**: Use technical terms when communicating with industry professionals (fuel types, equipment specifications) ### Words to Avoid - Vague promises without specifics - Overly casual language that undermines professionalism - Negative language or comparisons with competitors - Clichés that don't reflect our authentic brand voice ## Formatting ### Headlines - Use sentence case for most headlines - Keep headlines concise and descriptive - Use questions sparingly ### Lists - Use bullet points for unordered lists - Use numbered lists for sequential information - Keep list items parallel in structure ## Tone by Context - **Marketing Materials**: Professional, service-focused, emphasizing reliability and availability - **Customer Communications**: Clear, helpful, and reassuring - emphasize our 24/7 availability - **Emergency Response Communications**: Urgent but professional, emphasizing our disaster response capabilities - **Technical Documentation**: Clear, precise, and instructional - **Social Media**: Professional yet approachable, highlighting our services and customer success stories - **Website Content**: Service-oriented, clear about what we offer and how we can help ## Key Phrases and Terminology ### Always Use - "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™" (our tagline) - "24/7, 365 days a year" (when describing availability) - "Ensuring maximum performance with no downtime" - "Professional services you can count on" - "Going the extra mile" ### Service Descriptions - **Fuel Services**: "Bulk fuel delivery," "same-day fuel delivery," "fuel distribution" - **Disaster Response**: "Emergency response services," "disaster response," "24/7 emergency fuel service" - **Lubricants**: "Specialized lubricant delivery," "high-quality lubricants" - **Additional Services**: "Portable toilet rentals," "roll-off dumpster rentals," "bulk water services"
