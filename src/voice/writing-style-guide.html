--- layout: layouts/base.njk title: Writing Style Guide description: Guidelines for written communication breadcrumbs: - label: Home url: / - label: Voice url: /voice - label: Writing Style Guide url: /voice/writing-style-guide category: Voice --- ## Writing Style Guide This guide provides specific rules and recommendations for written communications. ## Grammar and Style ### Active Voice Prefer active voice over passive voice for clarity and impact. **Good**: "We deliver exceptional service." **Avoid**: "Exceptional service is delivered by us." ### Sentence Length Keep sentences concise and clear. Aim for 15-20 words on average. ### Paragraph Length Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences) for better readability, especially online. ## Word Choice ### Preferred Terms - Use clear, simple language - Avoid jargon unless your audience understands it - Choose specific words over vague ones ### Words to Avoid - Industry jargon (unless necessary) - Overly technical terms (unless appropriate) - Clich√©s and buzzwords ## Formatting ### Headlines - Use sentence case for most headlines - Keep headlines concise and descriptive - Use questions sparingly ### Lists - Use bullet points for unordered lists - Use numbered lists for sequential information - Keep list items parallel in structure ## Tone by Context - **Marketing**: Engaging and inspiring - **Support**: Helpful and empathetic - **Documentation**: Clear and instructional - **Social Media**: Conversational and authentic
