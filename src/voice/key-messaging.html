--- layout: layouts/base.njk title: Key Messaging description: Core messages and brand positioning breadcrumbs: - label: Home url: / - label: Voice url: /voice - label: Key Messaging url: /voice/key-messaging category: Voice --- ## Key Messaging Our key messages communicate who we are, what we do, and why Moffitt Services is the trusted choice for fuel, lubricants, and emergency response services. ## Core Messages ### Primary Message **"Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™"** This tagline encapsulates our core philosophy: we are a service company first, committed to ensuring maximum performance with no downtime. ### Value Proposition Moffitt Services provides professional services you can count on. We offer same-day fuel delivery, 24/7, 365 days a year, ensuring your vehicles and equipment will always have fuel - guaranteed. No matter where you're located, our team goes that extra mile to ensure you get the best service possible. ### Mission Statement To provide quality fuels, products, lubricants, and superior service to customers across a wide array of industries, ensuring maximum performance with no downtime. We are committed to being a nationally-based professional service provider that customers can rely on. ### Vision Statement To be recognized as the most trusted and reliable professional service provider in the fuel distribution and emergency response industries, known for exceptional service, reliability, and going the extra mile for every customer. ## Message Hierarchy 1. **Primary Message** - The main point you want to communicate 2. **Supporting Messages** - Additional points that reinforce the primary message 3. **Proof Points** - Evidence and examples that support your messages ## Usage Guidelines - Lead with your primary message - Support with secondary messages - Use proof points to build credibility - Adapt messaging for different audiences - Maintain consistency across channels ## Audience-Specific Messaging ### For Customers Emphasize reliability, availability (24/7, 365), and our guarantee that equipment will always have fuel. Highlight our comprehensive services: fuel delivery, lubricants, disaster response, and sanitary solutions. Use language that conveys trust and professionalism. ### For Partners Focus on our experience since 1947, national reach, and reputation as a highly respected distributor. Emphasize our commitment to quality and service excellence. Highlight our ability to serve the lower 48 states. ### For Internal Teams Reinforce our core values: service excellence, reliability, and going the extra mile. Emphasize our tagline "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™" as a guiding principle. Communicate our commitment to ensuring maximum performance with no downtime.
