These guidelines ensure consistent and proper usage across all applications, from fuel delivery trucks to marketing materials, website, and emergency response vehicles. ## General Principles 1. Always use approved logo files from the [Download](/download/logo-files) section 2. Maintain clear space around the logo to preserve its integrity 3. Never alter or modify the logo, including the tagline "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™" 4. Use appropriate logo variations for different contexts (full color, single color, reversed) 5. Ensure sufficient contrast for visibility, especially on vehicle graphics and outdoor signage 6. The logo must be clearly visible on all service vehicles, equipment, and uniforms ## Primary Logo The primary Moffitt Services logo is the main representation of our brand and should be used in most applications, including website headers, business cards, letterhead, marketing materials, and vehicle graphics. ### Logo Specifications

Logo Display

The Moffitt Services logo includes the company name and may include the tagline "Service is our business. We just happen to sell fuel.™"

Note: Actual logo files should be obtained from the Brand Team or the Download section.

Never stretch, compress, or distort the logo. ## Do's and Don'ts Follow these guidelines to ensure proper logo usage and avoid common mistakes. ### Do's ✓

Correct Usage

  • Use approved logo files from the [Download](/download/logo-files) section
  • Maintain proper clear space around the logo
  • Use the logo at appropriate sizes
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for visibility
  • Use the correct color variation for the background
  • Scale the logo proportionally
### Don'ts ✗

Incorrect Usage

  • Don't alter or modify the logo
  • Don't change the colors
  • Don't rotate or skew the logo
  • Don't add effects like shadows, gradients, or outlines
  • Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds without proper treatment
  • Don't use outdated logo versions
  • Don't recreate or redraw the logo
### Common Mistakes #### Incorrect Color Usage Never use the logo in unapproved colors or color combinations.
#### Improper Spacing Always maintain the required clear space around the logo.
#### Distortion Never stretch, compress, or distort the logo proportions.
#### Effects and Filters Avoid adding drop shadows, glows, or other effects to the logo.
